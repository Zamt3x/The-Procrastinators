Marvin's 3D model

The 3D model of Marvin was made by the user safa9raja3 and was gotten from Free3D.com.

Further texture modifications were done by us, and rigging + animations were gotten from Adobe's Mixamo.

Marvin's brain

His brain uses modified data files from Richard Wallace (drwallace) and is gotten from GitHub.

Marvin's brain also uses medical data about symptoms connected to illnesses, gotten from people.dbmi.columbia.edu.
